Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice combined with various ingredients such as seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits. It can be served in various forms, including rolls (maki), slices (nigiri), or in a bowl. Known for its delicate flavors and artistic presentation, sushi is a popular and healthy meal choice.
